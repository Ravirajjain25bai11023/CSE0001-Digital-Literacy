# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students
## Do’s
- **Think before you post** — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.  
- **Maintain a professional tone**, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.  
- **Protect your privacy** — avoid sharing personal or sensitive information.  
- **Verify information before sharing** to prevent spreading misinformation.  
- **Be respectful in comments and discussions**, even during disagreements.  

## Don’ts
- **Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.**  
- **Don’t share fake news or unverified information.**  
- **Don’t overshare personal details** like address, phone number, etc.  
- **Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling.**  
- **Don’t ignore your digital footprint** — what you post stays online.
