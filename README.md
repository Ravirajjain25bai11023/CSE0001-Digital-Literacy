## 📚 Module Summaries

### 🎨 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Designed a one-page infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy, its importance, and safe internet practices. This task enhanced my ability to communicate ideas visually and effectively.

### 💼 Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
Created and updated professional profiles on **GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle**. This helped me understand the importance of building a strong digital identity and showcasing my skills online.

### 💻 Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Solved a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank and created a **Google Form quiz** for digital literacy awareness. This improved my problem-solving skills and introduced me to collaborative tools.

### ✉️ Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Social Media Guide
Drafted professional emails and created a **social media do’s and don’ts checklist**. This task strengthened my formal communication and digital responsibility skills.

### 🔐 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Developed a case study on **UPI fraud** and created a **prevention checklist**. This task increased my awareness of online threats and safe digital practices.
