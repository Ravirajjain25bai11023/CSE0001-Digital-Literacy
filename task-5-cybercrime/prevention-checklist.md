# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

## General Safety Tips
- **Use strong, unique passwords** for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- **Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links** received through SMS, email, or social media.
- **Keep your devices updated** with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
- **Do not use public Wi-Fi** for sensitive activities such as banking or accessing personal accounts.
- **Be mindful of what you share online** to protect your digital footprint and personal information.
- **Verify the identity of individuals** before sharing any personal, academic, or confidential details.

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- **Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking credentials** with anyone.
- **Remember: receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN.**
- **Always verify payment requests carefully** before approving them.
- **Avoid screen-sharing apps** for banking or payment-related assistance.

## Reporting and Immediate Action
- **Report cyber fraud immediately** at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- **Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** for urgent assistance
- **Inform your bank promptly** to block or prevent unauthorized transactions
